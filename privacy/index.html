Privacy Policy

Effective July 13, 2026

The short version: every calculator on MapleCalcs runs entirely in your browser. The numbers you type — your mortgage balance, income, estate value — are never sent to us, never collected, and never stored. We couldn't see them if we wanted to.

What we collect

MapleCalcs has no user accounts, no sign-up forms, and no databases of visitor information. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any financial figures you enter into our tools. All calculations execute locally on your device using JavaScript.

Advertising and cookies

To keep our tools free, this site displays advertising served by Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your browsing.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of some third-party vendors' cookies at www.aboutads.info. For more on how Google uses data from sites that use its services, see policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Affiliate disclosure

Some pages may contain affiliate links to financial products or services. If you follow such a link and complete a transaction, MapleCalcs may earn a commission at no cost to you. Affiliate relationships never influence the formulas, rates, or figures our calculators produce — those come from statutes, government publications, and standard financial mathematics, with the method documented on each page.

Analytics

We do not currently run any analytics or tracking scripts of our own. If that changes, this policy will be updated before any such tool is deployed.

Your rights

MapleCalcs is operated from Canada and respects the principles of the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). Since we collect no personal information ourselves, there is generally nothing for us to access, correct, or delete — but if you have any privacy question or concern, contact us and we will respond.

Contact

Questions about this policy: hello@maplecalcs.org

Changes

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
